Enemy AI Showcase

Unity: C#
Enemy AI that I made for showcase. It features stack based finite state machine at its core.

Features: FOV, attacking, pursuing enemy, flanking enemy, supporting allies, search for enemy, dynamic roles and groups

Enemy Group: The enemy belongs in a group of enemies that are in close proximity to each other. Enemies in a group are able to communicate with each other.

Enemy Roles: Attackers, Flankers and Supports. The Master AI Controller assigns roles dynamically during runtime.
